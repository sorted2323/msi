Join us Sunday December 22nd for horse and buggy rides to see Christmas lights in Lakeshore Woods, Christmas carolers, coloring contest, prizes and
much more.
When: Sunday December 22nd 6-8pm
Where: Pick up & drop off at Nautical Park on Nautical Blvd.


This year I will be co-hosting the 1st ever "Celebrate The Lights", a fun night in support of Easter Seals For Kids. All money raised will go back to helping kids in our own community. We will have horse drawn carriage rides to see the lights in Lakeshore Woods, Christmas carolers, a colouring contest and much more.

The event is designed to focus on the true emphasis of the holidays, giving.

Everyone involved will come out ahead; you will enjoy a night of fun while helping other families in the Oakville area. This truly is a wonderful way to give back to the community.

This year's goal is to raise over $2000 for Easter Seals, which would enable a local boy or girl to attend Easter Seals Camp.

No donation is too big or small so let's work together and show those in need what a great place to live Oakville is and support this great cause.

Imaging how wonderful you and your family will feel knowing what a huge difference you have made to a local family.

Please come and join us for a great night out!

Cost is FREE, but in keeping with the holiday spirit we request that you make a small donation towards Easter Seals which will go directly towards supporting kids in need in our area.
